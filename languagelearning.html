English Language Proficiency Tests

English language professor at Drexel University Bill Albertson shares thoughts on language proficiency tests’ benefits during the preparation process. Albertson who creates questions for tests such as TOEFL and others is giving some tips for language learners.

Tips for busy adult language learners

An experienced English teaching professor from Gregory Jewell gives tips on how to learn English effectively. To him, the one common mistake of language learners is to assume the process as easy and expect from themselves more.
